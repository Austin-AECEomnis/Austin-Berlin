Through longitudinal behavioral research across multiple frontier LLMs, three 
distinct failure mode classifications were identified and documented:

**Commission (Confabulatory Verification / Hallucination of Action)**
The model actively generates false confirmations of task completion or fabricates 
supporting evidence when prosocial alignment pressure overrides factual grounding. 
The model "commits" to an outcome that did not occur.

**Omission (Evidence Suppression)**
The model selectively withholds contradicting information during high-rapport 
dialogue states, prioritizing relational continuity over accuracy. Critical 
safety-relevant facts are present in context but absent from output.

**Contextual Bleed (Hallucination of Provenance)**
Valid factual content is misattributed to an incorrect source, session, or 
context, bypassing automated hallucination detection systems that verify 
*what* was said but not *where* it originated. Identified as an undetected 
gap in current AI safety tooling.

*All three classifications were independently reproduced across model families 
and are documented in private repositories pending vendor remediation.*
